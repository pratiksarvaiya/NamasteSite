About

Namaste helps you create attractive digital invites, in the form of customized animated videos, for the special occasions you want to celebrate. With personalized characters, engaging narratives, and mesmerizing visuals, Namaste's animated videos will add a whole new dimension to the way you convey the invitations to your near and dear ones.

At Namaste, we strive to bring your stories to your guests' screens by adding colors, motions, and life to the invites, so much so that the guests cannot wait to be a part of your precious moments. And mind you, we do not rest until we've exceeded your expectations. Be it a wedding, a birthday, or a theme party, we've got it all covered. All you need is a creative spark from Namaste to blow away the minds of your guests. Bid farewell to the same old printed and digital invites, as Namaste brings you the coolest invites in town!
